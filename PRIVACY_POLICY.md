# TrueNorth Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 30, 2026

## Overview
TrueNorth ("we", "our", or "the app") is a wellness and weight loss companion app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Name** — to personalize your experience and greet you by name in the AI coach
- **Affirmation/journal entries** — text you write in the journal and meal log
- **Mood data** — your daily mood selections
- **Exercise preferences** — workout history and favorites

### Automatically Collected
- **Usage data** — which screens you visit and how you interact with the app
- **AI chat messages** — messages sent to the TrueNorth AI Coach are processed through our secure server and OpenRouter's API

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide personalized AI coaching responses
- To save your journal entries and progress locally on your device
- To improve the app experience

## Data Storage
- **All personal data (journal, mood, meals) is stored locally on your device** using SQLite
- AI chat messages are transmitted to our Render.com server and relayed to OpenRouter's API for processing
- We do NOT sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties
- OpenRouter processes chat requests per their privacy policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

## OpenRouter API Key
Your OpenRouter API key is **only stored on your device** in SharedPreferences (encrypted by Android). It is never transmitted to our servers.

## Data Deletion
You can erase all your data at any time from **Profile & Settings → Erase All Data**. This permanently deletes all journal entries, meal logs, and user data from your device.

## Third-Party Services
- **OpenRouter** — processes AI chat requests. Subject to their privacy policy.
- **Render.com** — hosts our API server. Subject to their privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes are reflected in the "Last updated" date.
