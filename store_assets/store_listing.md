## Full Description

TrueNorth is a wellness app built on a simple belief: you don't need to be smaller to be healthier. Designed for plus-size bodies, TrueNorth offers a shame-free space where movement feels good, nourishment comes without guilt, and self-care is never a punishment.

### What makes TrueNorth different?

**Shame-Free Language**
No "cheat days." No "bad foods." No guilt trips. TrueNorth uses compassionate language that celebrates progress over perfection.

**Movement Library**
Choose from 17 guided exercise videos designed for real bodies — from seated stretches to gentle flows and energizing workouts. Each video includes modifications so you can move at your own pace.

**AI Coach**
Your personal wellness companion that learns your name, celebrates your wins, and offers kind, practical advice. Powered by AI with a warm, human touch.

**Nourish Log**
Track meals without judgment. Simple logging to help you notice patterns — not to count every calorie.

**Journal**
Daily prompts to help you check in with yourself. Write about gratitude, challenges, and everything in between.

**SOS Grounding Tools**
Feeling overwhelmed? Use our 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique, deep breathing exercises, and body scan to find calm.

**Progress Dashboard**
See your steps, mindful minutes, meals logged, and journal entries — all in one beautiful, motivating view.

### Who is TrueNorth for?

TrueNorth is for people who are tired of wellness culture that makes them feel broken. It's for every body — at every size, every fitness level, every stage of the journey.

### Features:
- 17 guided exercise videos (seated, low-impact, and joyful movement)

- Shame-free meal and mood tracking
